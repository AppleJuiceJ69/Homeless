What Is PRH

PRH is a Florida Corporation Street Ministry located in St Augustine that Darrel and I registered after we were married in 2008. We began helping with preparing meals for "Dining with Dignity", located on Bridge Street, every Friday evening. We became associated with the many other groups that assist with the homeless including churches and restaurants. We have never been a giant organization. We have always done our best to be Jesus's hands and feet by assisting one person at a time depending on the circumstances.
Assisting with rent, picking up medicine, motel stays, Dr bills, groceries, etc. We have acted many times as the middleman in sharing things such as houses, furniture, clothing, cars, motorcycles, bikes, money. Etc. actually it is given to PRH, then God finds a home for it and it's amazing how fast he works on these connections. My husband Darrel became ill in 2018 and had repeated episodes of hospital stays, rehab and good times at home until he just could not recover the fourth time. He was faithful till the end in his dedication to those on the streets on St. Augustine. I feel Jesus telling me to continue the PRH mission in Darrels remembrance by assisting people in their time of need. My new mission will be to continue using money giving to PRH to support the 5013c3 organizations we have been assisting from the beginning.
